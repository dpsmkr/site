#00 Hello

My name is Jonas and I also go by the pseudonym DPSMKR. I'm a brazilian sound and visual artist that work mainly with digital media. I've produced ambient, drone and experimental music for the past 10 years, and recently am entering more deeply into code and procedural / generative art. Feel free to contact me via dpsmkr@gmail.com or twitter.com/dpsmkr_
