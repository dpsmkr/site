Hello

My name is Jonas and I also go by the pseudonym DPSMKR. I'm a sound and visual artist from Brazil who works mainly with digital audio. I've produced ambient, drone and experimental music for over the past 10 years, and recently am entering more deeply into code and procedural / generative art.
Feel free to contact me via dpsmkr@gmail.com or twitter.com/dpsmkr_

Sound works

Ambient, experimental and drone music projects.

  • DPSMKR (2011-)

    • Minimalistic improvisational guitar loops and feedback noise project.

  • Cruise Noir (2017-)

    • Computer music project. Loosely based on video game music and Studio Ghibli soundtracks.

  • AKAAO (2018-)

    • Synthesizer music. Recorded mainly with KORG Volca, pure data and Automatonism.

  • Cortina de Fumaça (2018-)

    • Drone duo project with Daniel Hogrefe.
